Tournament Rules:

  1. Each team needs to publish 13 players before the First game of the tournament
  2. Captains needs to discuss the time and place of the game in the scheduled week
  3. Home team is responsible to secure the court and provide atleast 3 time slot to Away team
  4. Games can be completed in the schedule week or before the schedule week. After the Cutoff date it will be marked as Notplayed or Forefiet
  5. Each match will be a 3 set with 21 21 21 points game
  6. Team winning the match will get 4 points and team winning one set will get 2 points and playing the match will get 1 point
  7. Regular volleryball rules. Please discuss with opponent captain before the start of the game for any clarifications
  8. Teams needs to get their volleyball
  9. Top 4 teams will play semi's and Finals . Both Semi's and Final's happen on the same day.
  10. If 3 teams tie up, then NRR(net run rate) will be taken . If two teams tie up then winner of the league will be ranked above.
  11. All games must be played on the Sand Volleyball courts unless both team agrees to play indoor. *BOTH TEAMS MUST ACCEPT. Default is Sand Volleyball
  12. No court dimensions. Teams can decide and play
  13. Tournament organizers are not responsible for any accidents/injuries/incidents that happen to or between the players in the game, all we are doing is facilitating a group for like minded people to have fun.
  14. If you are playing in this Tournament that means you are agreeing to the above point and agreeing that organizer or any of the participants are not liable to accidents/injuries/incidents that may happen.
